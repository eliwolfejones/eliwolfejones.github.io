Soft Pretzels

Savory, salted soft pretzels which are great as a party snack!

Ingredients

  • Pretzel Ingredients
    • 2 and 1/4 teaspoons instant yeast
    • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
    • 1 and 1/2 cups warm water
    • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
    • 1 teaspoon salted
    • 4 cups all-pourpose flour
    • Coarse salt, for topping
  • Baking Soda Bath Ingredients
    • 9 cups water
    • 1/2 cup baking soda
