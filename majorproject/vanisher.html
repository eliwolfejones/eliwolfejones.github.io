Vanisher, Horizon Scraper

Vanisher, Horizon Scraper is Quadeca's latest album and continues his exploration into experimental and alternative music. The album follows a story of a sailor running from the real world where he experiences the consequences of avoiding his problems with isolation. This is shown in the viuals that were released at the time of the album and the lyricism of songs such as Waging War and Forgone.
