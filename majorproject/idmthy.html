I Didn't Mean To Haunt You

I Didn't Mean To Haunt You was the project that demonstrated his full transformation adn showed what Quadeca as an artist was capable of. It told a story about a ghost who had commited suicide but instead of going to the afterlife he is stuck in a purgatory where he witnesses the affects of his death on people close to him. "Fantasyworld" serves as a prequel which shows his thoughts before his passing. He is forced to witness lives and people and their experiences without him leading to a reflection on his life and the regrets he has but also eventually the frusteration due to some of his family trying not to think about his death which he sees as disrespectful. This frusteration and sadness turns causes him to show signs that he is there but he gets reckless through his frusteration and he unintentionally turns up the stove releasing carbon monoxide which Danny Brown does lyrics for on "House Settling" which causes his mother to also pass. The conclusion of the album leads him to finally pass into a world beyond as he confesses to his mother everything that has been on his consious including how she passed.
