Other Projects

Blush by Kevin Abstract + More

Blush is a collaborative album led by Kevin Abstract, featuring contributions from various artists including Dominic Fike, Love Spells, JPEGMAFIA, and Quadeca. Quadeca's involvement reflects his experimental sound as it crosses over from his own albums on to Blush. The songs he has production and/or writing credits on include "Copy," "NOLA," "Post Break Up Beauty," "97 Jag," "Blush Interlude," "Pop Out," "Abandon Me," and "Red Light." It is also the first album co-released under Quadeca's solo record label called "X8 Music."

Running Running Running by Johan Lenox

Running Running Running is a song by Johan Lenox which Quadeca helped write and produce. The song is about the fallout of a relationship which the main character saw as their world and only social connection. Quadeca's vocals symbolize the character trying to reach out to their former lover hoping to fix their relationshipo but ultimately being consumed by emotion and losing control.

Stardust by Danny Brown

Stardust is Danny Brown's sixth studio album which symbolizes a new era of Danny's life and the hardships he went through in the past to become who he is today. Danny Brown appeared on I Didn't Mean To Haunt on the track "House Settling" You sparking the beginning of Quadeca and Danny working together. He then appeared on "The Great Bakunawa" from Vanisher, Horizon Scraper. Quadeca is credited to have co-produced and co-written the songs "Book of Daniel" and "What You See" on Stardust. He also contributed his own vocals to both songs.

Sunshine When It Rains/See The World by Olēka

Olēka is an up-and-coming artist who appeard on Vanisher, Horizon Scraper on the track "Waging War." Soon after Quadeca released Vanisher, Horizon Scraper Olēka released her debut single "Sunshine When It Rains/See The World" under Quadeca's record label X8 Music.
