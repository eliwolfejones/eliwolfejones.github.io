Albums

From Me To You

From Me To You was the first album that really saw Quadeca in the start of his musical transformation. This album uses a theme of climbing a snowy mountain and the metaphors about challenges that that it symbolizes through the visuals that accompany it and songs like Sisyphus and Alone Together.

I Didn't Mean To Haunt You

I Didn't Mean To Haunt You represented Quadeca's full transition into experimental and alternative music. It follows a story about a ghost who is stuck in a purgatory in between life and death and the regrets about what he should have done during his life. This is reflected throught the visuals and songs compiled in the album such as Born Yesterday and Fractions of Infinity.

Scrapyard

Scrapyard was a compilation of songs that Quadeca made in between I Didn't Mean To Haunt You and Vanisher, Horizon Scraper which he didn't feel fit either albums. The songs to not reflect an apparent theme like the others but visuals he release with the album followed the theme of a scrapyard much like the title suggests.

Vanisher, Horizon Scraper

Vanisher, Horizon Scraper is Quadeca's latest album and continues his exploration into experimental and alternative music. The album follows a story of a sailor running from the real world where he experiences the consequences of avoiding his problems with isolation. This is shown in the viuals that were released at the time of the album and the lyricism of songs such as Waging War and Forgone.
