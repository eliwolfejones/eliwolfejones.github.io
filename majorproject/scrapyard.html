Scrapyard

Scrapyard was a compilation of songs that Quadeca made in between I Didn't Mean To Haunt You and Vanisher, Horizon Scraper which he didn't feel fit either albums. The songs to not reflect an apparent theme like the others but visuals he release with the album followed the theme of a scrapyard much like the title suggests.
