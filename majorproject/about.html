About Quadeca

Quadeca, born Benjamin Lasky, is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, and YouTuber. He first gained popularity through his YouTube channel, where he posted music reviews and original songs. Over time, Quadeca transitioned from being primarily a YouTuber to a full-time musician, releasing several albums and singles that showcase his versatility and talent in the hip-hop genre.
