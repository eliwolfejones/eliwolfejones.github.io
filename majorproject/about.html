About Quadeca

Quadeca, born Benjamin Lasky, is an American singer, songwriter, and producer. He first gained popularity through his YouTube channel, where he posted music reviews and original songs. Over time, Quadeca transitioned from being primarily a YouTuber to a full-time musician, releasing several albums and singles that showcase his versatility and talent in the hip-hop genre.

When releasing From Me To You he expereimented with new sounds different from his signature rep style. After positive feedback to this expereimental sound he created his purly experimental indie album I Didn't Mean To Haunt You showing his versatility and potential in this new genre. Scrapyard was another example of how his skill spans multiple genres and he also collabed with people in those genres showing his dynamic with other artists that don't solely rap.

Before releasing Vanisher, Horizon Scraper he collaborated with a lot more artists who were interested in his experimental sound further expanding and exposing his expertise. This was once again shown in his latest album Vanisher, Horizon Scraper where he pushed the boundaries of his musical talent further.
