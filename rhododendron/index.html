Pacific Rhododendron

>

Alternate Names

  • Coast Rhododendron
  • California Rosebay
  • Big Leaf Rhododendron

The Pacific Rhododendron can range from heights of 3 to 29 feet and widths of 5 to 15. feet

The Pacific Rhododendron can be found natively west of the Cascade Mountains from southern British Columbia to northern California.

It thrices in temperate, moist to dry climates.
